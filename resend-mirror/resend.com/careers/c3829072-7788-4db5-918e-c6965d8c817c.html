Open Source Engineer

Americas / Remote / Full-time

Resend is growing fast, and there are now over 1 million developers using our product. We're looking for an Open Source Engineer to help build and maintain our developer tools ecosystem. This role will be crucial in creating high-quality, open source packages that enhance our platform's developer experience and community engagement.

Read more about how we work, how we hire, and what we value here.

In this role you will...

  • Build and maintain react.email for developers to integrate with/without Resend

  • Design and implement developer tools that simplify building emails

  • Contribute to open standards and collaborate with the developer community

  • Document and showcase integration patterns and best practices

  • Triage and resolve issues in our public repositories and engage with contributors

You will be a perfect fit if you...

  • Have 5 years of software engineering experience

  • Are fluent in writing and speaking English

  • Can consistently work in Americas timezone

  • You love helping other developers

You will be an exceptional fit if you also...

  • Have experience maintaining open source projects and managing community contributions

  • Have deep experience with TypeScript and multiple programming languages

  • Are skilled at writing clear technical documentation

  • Have experience with CI/CD for package publishing and release automation

What it means to join the team:

  • Autonomy to "just ship it"

  • 100% remote team with flexible working schedules

  • Modern tech stack

  • Honest and low-ego team

  • Ownership of problems and solutions

About Resend

We are building the modern email sending platform for developers. We care deeply about quality, creating for everyone and building in the open.

Our fully remote team of 30 humans spans 9 countries... and counting. We’re backed by a16z, Y Combinator, Basecase, and other top investors.
